Models History About Us

1963 - Deluxe

Our first car, Deluxe was set out in the market in 1963. It featured advanced tech, way ahead of time. The car was built for speed, along with fuel optimization.

1977 - Trident OT-45

The Trident was built to feature high performance, which allowed for a great top speed and acceleration. We had released many variants of this car, and also allowed various customization possibilites.

1984 - Eagle Machine

The Eagle was our first vehicle in which we introduced various technologies such as radio, mini-telivision and a fax machine. It was built for premium luxury.

2004 - Wagon T12

We had built the Wagon to have immense horse power. Computing devices were installed for fuel and performance optimization, allowing for greater usage of the inherent horse power.

2007 - Vision GTRS

Our first supercar, Vision GTRS enabled us to dive into the supercar regime and compete with the best out there.

2011 - Venom Supersport

The Venom was built specially for extremely high performance. It achieved a top speed of 290 miles per hour, beating most of the competitors of it's time. Along with such speed, it also featured initial AI assisting capabilities and AI based performance enhancement.

2019 - Hypersport GT

The Hypersport GT achieved a top speed of 340 miles per hour. It features AI based aerodynamic shift, advanced assist capabilities and extreme performance modes. Advanced Track mode allows for superior performance than most competitors.
