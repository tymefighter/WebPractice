Windu Carlight

Founder and CEO

Windu Carlight was passionate about cars since childhood. It was his dream to combine performance, luxury and technology, and introduce them into building great cars. The first car built by Extreme Motors was Deluxe, it was completely designed and engineered by Windu himself.

Executive Team

Our Executive team features extremely talented leaders from around the globe. The decisions of the executive team have lead to Extreme Motors being what it is today.

Core Engineering Team

Our Core Engineering team has engineers from various domains, from Mechanical Engineers up to AI and Machine Learning Engineers. The extensive research and designing done by the core engineering team has led to our cars having great performance, luxury and technology.
